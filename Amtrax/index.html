About Amtrax

Amtrax is a privately owned Human Resource Consultancy and Recruitment firm which has been set up to meet the needs of businesses that may not have the resources to have a dedicated Human Resources professional but require the services that benefit the organisation or businesses who may have specific HR projects they need help with. The mission was to become a full service HR outsourcing company offering HR solutions that could be customised and tailored to meet the needs of employers of all sizes.

Our Story

Drawing on near 30 years HR experience across our team, Amtrax found that many of the employers needed more than a HR advice line; they needed expert to handle their HR for them. SMEs wanted to be able to pass over key HR tasks and issues to an experienced practitioner who could identify the right solution to resolve issues; giving them peace of mind and HR insights as they handled other aspects of their businesses. Amtrax provide practical and business related advice in order for clients to run their business to its optimum. Amtrax is growing to be an established HR and Recruitment firm with a team of experienced Consultants, who provide excellent HR support to organisations of all sizes. Amtrax growth as a business stem from its ability to adapt services to each individual clients needs and requirements. Since the beginning, Amtrax have delivered a truly customised HR service for every client. Everything Amtrax does, from the services it provides to the relationships built, is influenced by desire to deliver Human Resources with the human touch.

Organisation Development

Our team of experienced HR and OD consultants can partner with you to deliver sustained improvement and change as your business grows and evolves

Organisation development is about improving the effectiveness of your business through your people. It’s based on the principle that many elements of an organisation are interlinked and therefore a holistic, and wider approach, can identify the real issues and enable lasting change.

The skill of organisational development (OD) is identifying the key factors (i.e. what to rule out, what to include), influencing productivity and making key (often quite small) changes to fine tune systems, processes and the way that our people work together. This enables us to deliver sustained improvement as our organisations continue to grow and evolve

Our team of experienced HR and organisation development consultants will partner with you to help you build and maintain a sustainable organisation

So, whether you’re dealing with a current HR issue, or you’re keen to proactively explore how best to support your organisation’s productivity in the future, organisation development solutions can unlock hidden obstacles, and provide clarity on what’s important.

Employee Survey

Employee engagement is a significant driver for organisational success. How you measure this depends on a number of factors, but a good quality staff survey will give you valuable insights.
